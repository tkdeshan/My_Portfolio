Project Details :

  • Name : Self Assistant AI Web App(In progress)
  • About : The AI Career Guidance and Knowledge Testing Web Application is a versatile platform that combines a chat interface and dashboard to offer personalized career advice and knowledge testing. Users can interact with AI to explore career options, take quizzes, and receive tailored guidance based on their interests and skills. The dashboard provides a convenient summary of their testing results for easy tracking and improvement. Overall, it's a comprehensive tool for individuals navigating their career paths.
